Overview


One of life’s universal experiences is moving from one place to another. Our research suggests that this experience is stressful, time-consuming and overwhelming.

Swimming in the pool of the Moving Services Market, we found good reviews and very bad reviews from an enormous list of Moving Companies in the ratio of Texas, one of them was this company that grabbed our attention Einstein Moving Company.

Einstein Moving Company, in 2018 the Austin Chronicle considered the Best Movers in Austin, and also have very good reviews in Yelp! and other Social Media pages, their Website is easy to navigate and the response is immediately if you decide to make use of their services or have some doubts.

Challenge

Design a mobile application for a Moving Service Company , our goal was to add value and improve the user experience, this App was considered for People who are trying to move from their resent household to a new one, but needs a little help and orientation to do it, a quick process and intuitive navigation is our objective. Media pages, their Website is easy to navigate and the response is immediately if you decide to make use of their services or have some doubts.

Metodology

In order to undestand customers and their problems and explore the best practices in innovatives way to solve them we use the Design Thinking Metodology. Guided with the Double Diamond structure, we learn how to Discover, Define, Develop and Deliver a Mobile Application that can improve the user experience.

Discover

Top Competitor

College H.U.N.K.S. is a Moving company that offers innovation services,whether you hire them for junk hauling, moving, or end-to-end services. Have competitve prices and a very flexible dinamic with the user.

Streghts

-Moving (local, long distance, offices).

-Junk removal including hoarding.

-Has same day availability for picking up junk.

Weaknesses

-Strong competitor but lacks mobile app.

-Chat feature directs you to a bot, not a live person.

-No human touch.

Customers needs

During our research, we discovered that our user likes to hire a moving company that a friend or a colleague recommend , and so then relay on the company to pack and unpack their belongings, because planning and packing is a very stressful activity for them.
Therefore, we believe that our users would prefer hiring a reliable moving company that is well recommended and give budget offers that we might be able to help if we create an app that can help them in every step of the moving and recommend some of the thing they can help in the process.
Research & Interviews

Our interviewees expressed that it’s Overwhelming and Difficult to organize their move and that it can be a Time Consuming Process to research moving companies, obtain quotes and finalize a booking.

Survey Discoveries

We discover in the Survey Results, the importance of the Reviews when thinking on hiring a Moving Company, as well the time for planning is less than a month, and usually make a combination of Hiring and Do It Your Self to maintain a low budget and reduce stress of carrying heavy and big items like furniture.

Affinity Diagram Highlights


We collect all the data from our Research, Interviews and Surveys and sorted on an Affinity Map, to our key focus.

Moving Companies lose customers because of the pricing and poor or bad reviews of their services.

Also, we found 4 key words that define our user pain points about Moving are: Stressful and Overwhelming because Moving is time consuming for most of the users.

Most of the users have a lot of trouble organizing and they do not know where to start so they use a small checklist to keep up with some things.
