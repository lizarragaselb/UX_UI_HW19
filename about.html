Hi, I'm Sofia Lizarraga

UX/UI Designer


After more than 8 years of experience, developing Research projects, Industrial and Web Design for different industries, mainly for construction, collaborating with the Government and the private area, I had the opportunity to learn and meet the user from different perspectives analyzing problems , real needs, intelligent and innovative solutions to them, and as a result tangible and functional projects for customers and users.

In the beginning of my career I learned to Research and Development projects through various Methodologies and Tools such as Design Thinking, and the importance of developing Intelligent Designs that really meet the needs of users, this is how I learned the importance of UX/UI Design and decided to take and complete the UX/UI Design Bootcamp at the University of Texas at Austin.

For me the most important thing is to design experiences for users that are really Functional, Intuitive and Inclusive.
